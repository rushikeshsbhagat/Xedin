Download or Stream Movies and TV via torrent into One App.

Xedin fetches data from TMDB and lets you stream content from various Fembed sites
with its on customized WebView, or fetch and stream torrents directly.

Features

Stream Media from Sources like VidSrc, VideoSpider, 123Files, GDrive Player and Free Streaming in a customized Webview that blocks all pop-ups.
Fetch Torrent Results for any Media from TPB and YTS.
Stream Torrents on any external video player.
Completely Ad-Free.
Minimal UI, No Analytics bullshit.
Plus mode for TV that searches for torrent results for whole seasons (For healthier results) and lets you stream a specific episode.
Direct Streaming with External Player (Tap and hold Play Button).
